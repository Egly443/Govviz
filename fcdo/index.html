Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office

The most quantifiable lever of UK foreign policy: how much the UK spends on overseas development, against the statutory benchmark it set itself.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £8bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Aid spending (ODA) — UK official development assistance, % of gross national income (FCDO, Statistics on International Development)
  • Total ODA — UK net official development assistance, £ billion (FCDO, Statistics on International Development)
