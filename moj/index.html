Department for Justice

The throughput, cost, and capacity of the criminal-justice system. Hard to fudge: courts list cases publicly and prisons publish costs.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £13bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Crown Court outstanding caseload — Open cases awaiting trial or sentence (Ministry of Justice criminal court statistics)
  • Prison officer resignation rate — Band 3-5 voluntary resignations, annualised (HMPPS workforce statistics)
  • Average cost per prisoner — Direct + overheads, real terms £/yr (HMPPS annual report & accounts)
  • Crown Court case completion time — Median days from offence to completion (MoJ criminal court statistics quarterly)
