Cabinet Office

The centre of government is hard to score on outcomes — it coordinates rather than delivers — so the honest measures are how well the projects it oversees are delivering and how the size of the civil service is moving.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £2bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Major projects delivery confidence — % of Government Major Projects Portfolio rated green / amber-green (Infrastructure & Projects Authority, GMPP annual report)
  • Civil service headcount — Full-time-equivalent civil servants (Cabinet Office / ONS, Civil Service Statistics)
  • FOI requests answered in time — % of Freedom of Information requests answered within the statutory deadline (Cabinet Office, Freedom of Information statistics)
