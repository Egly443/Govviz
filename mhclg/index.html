Govviz / Housing, Communities & Local Government

Ministry of Housing, Communities & Local Government

Whether people can get and keep a roof: the supply of new homes, the affordability gap, and the households councils are housing in temporary accommodation.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £30bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Households in temporary accommodation — Households housed in TA at quarter-end, England (MHCLG statutory homelessness live tables (TA1))
  • Private rents — Price Index of Private Rents, UK (rebased index) (ONS Price Index of Private Rents (PIPR), historical series)
  • Council tax — Average Band D council tax, England (£/yr) (MHCLG, Council Tax levels set by local authorities (live tables))
  • Rough sleeping — People estimated sleeping rough on a single night, England (MHCLG, Rough sleeping snapshot in England)
  • New homes added — Net additional dwellings per year, England (MHCLG housing supply: net additional dwellings)
  • Housing affordability — Median house price ÷ median gross annual earnings, England (ONS house price to workplace-based earnings ratio)
  • Social housing waiting list — Households on local authority housing waiting lists, England (MHCLG live tables on rents, lettings and tenancies (Table 600))
