AI-ready open data

Govviz publishes every indicator as a reference implementation of its own AI-ready series profile: resolve a stable id → tidy CSV, with unit, coverage, periodicity, revision status, provenance and a published validation range in-band. No scraping, no tab-guessing, no wrong-but-plausible measure. Govviz is a downstream compiler; every record names its primary producer.

Catalogue: catalog.json (DCAT) · Graph: graph.jsonld · Profile: profile.json · Health history: health-history.json · Suppression scheme: v1 · Agent: MCP
