Govviz / Transport

Department for Transport

Daily reliability and long-term asset health: cancellations passengers see, agency backlogs drivers see, and capital programmes taxpayers pay for.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £30bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Passenger rail cancellation score — % of services cancelled (ORR cancellation score) (Office of Rail and Road, cancellation statistics)
  • Rail fares — CPI rail fares index — passenger transport by railway (2015 = 100) (ONS Consumer Prices Index — passenger transport by railway (D7EF))
  • Local road condition — % of local 'A' roads that should be considered for maintenance, England (DfT, Road condition statistics (RDC))
  • DVLA paper application backlog — Outstanding paper items in processing (DVLA performance data / Transport Committee)
  • DfT major projects delivery confidence — % of DfT GMPP projects rated Amber/Red or Red (IPA/NISTA Government Major Projects Portfolio)
  • Strategic road network condition — % of SRN pavement in good condition (target ≥96.2%) (Office of Rail and Road — annual assessment of National Highways (Table 3a))
  • Road deaths per 100,000 — Mortality from road traffic injury, per 100,000 — UK vs Germany & France (World Bank (WHO))
  • CO₂ emissions per person — Tonnes per person, per year — UK vs Germany & France (World Bank)
