Department for Energy Security & Net Zero

The decarbonisation trajectory the department is accountable for, alongside the energy security and affordability it must protect on the way.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £13bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Renewable energy share — Renewables as % of total final energy consumption (World Bank (IEA))
  • Greenhouse gas emissions — UK territorial emissions, million tonnes CO₂e (net zero by 2050) (DESNZ, Final UK greenhouse gas emissions national statistics)
  • Households in fuel poverty — % of English households (Low Income Low Energy Efficiency) (DESNZ, Annual Fuel Poverty Statistics)
  • Domestic electricity price — Average UK standard domestic electricity price, pence per kWh (DESNZ, Quarterly Energy Prices)
  • Energy use per person — Kg of oil equivalent per capita (World Bank (IEA))
