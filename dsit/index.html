Department for Science, Innovation & Technology

Whether the UK is investing in research at the intensity a high-productivity economy needs, and whether the digital infrastructure that growth runs on is reaching everyone.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £16bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • R&D spending — Gross domestic R&D expenditure (GERD), % of GDP (World Bank (OECD/UNESCO))
  • Researchers in R&D — Researchers per million people (World Bank (OECD/UNESCO))
  • Gigabit broadband coverage — % of UK premises with gigabit-capable broadband available (Ofcom, Connected Nations)
  • Internet users — % of individuals using the internet (World Bank (ITU))
  • Mobile subscriptions — Mobile-cellular subscriptions per 100 people (World Bank (ITU))
