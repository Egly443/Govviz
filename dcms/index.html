Department for Culture, Media & Sport

The economic and participation footprint of the sectors the department sponsors — creative industries, tourism and sport — which punch well above their budget line.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £2bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Inbound tourism — International tourist arrivals, millions (World Bank (UN Tourism))
  • Creative industries GVA — Gross value added of the creative industries, £ billion (DCMS, Economic Estimates: gross value added)
  • Adult sport participation — % of adults active (150+ minutes a week), Active Lives (Sport England, Active Lives Adult Survey)
