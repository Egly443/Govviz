Govviz / Business & Trade

Department for Business & Trade

The external-facing health of the economy the department promotes: how much the UK exports, how sophisticated those exports are, and whether business is investing.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £6bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Exports — Exports of goods & services, % of GDP (World Bank (ONS))
  • High-tech exports — % of manufactured exports (World Bank (UN Comtrade))
  • Business investment — UK business investment, £bn per quarter (chained volume, seasonally adjusted) (ONS, Business investment in the UK (series NPEL))
  • Retail sales — Retail sales volume index (2022 = 100, incl. fuel, seasonally adjusted) (ONS, Retail Sales Index (series J5EK))
  • Foreign direct investment — Net inflows, % of GDP (World Bank (IMF BoP))
