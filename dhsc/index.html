Govviz / Health & Social Care

Department for Health & Social Care

Decades of monthly data on how the department is performing against its stated objectives. Headline numbers in context, not in isolation.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £204bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Elective care waiting list — Incomplete RTT pathways (NHS England RTT, monthly)
  • 18-week treatment target compliance — % of incomplete pathways under 18 weeks (NHS England RTT, monthly)
  • Ambulance response (Category 2) — Mean response to emergency calls (heart attack, stroke), minutes (NHS England Ambulance Quality Indicators (AmbSYS))
  • Hospital discharge bottleneck — Beds/day occupied by patients medically fit for discharge (NHS England discharge delays, daily SitRep)
  • NHS temporary agency staff spend — Rolling 12-month, £ billion (NHS England board papers / NAO)
  • Capital programme cost overrun — Weighted variance across DHSC major projects (IPA Annual Report on Major Projects (GMPP))
  • Health spend per year of life — Annual health spend ($/person) ÷ life expectancy — lower = more longevity per $ (World Bank (WHO) ÷ ONS national life tables)
  • GP appointment access — % of patients with a good experience of making a GP appointment (GP Patient Survey) (NHS England / Ipsos, GP Patient Survey)
  • A&E 4-hour standard — % of attendances admitted/discharged within 4 hours (NHS England A&E attendances, monthly)
  • Clinical workforce per 1,000 people — Doctors vs nurses, per 1,000 population (World Bank (OECD/WHO))
  • Hospital beds per 1,000 people — Total care beds per 1,000 population — UK vs Germany & France (World Bank (OECD/WHO/Eurostat))
  • Health spending (% of GDP) — Total current health expenditure, % of GDP — UK vs Germany & France (World Bank (WHO/OECD))
  • Health spending per person — Current health expenditure, $ per person — UK vs Germany & France (World Bank (WHO))
  • Infant mortality — Deaths under age 1 per 1,000 live births (World Bank (UN IGME))
  • Suicide rate — Per 100,000 people (World Bank (WHO))
  • Measles immunisation — % of children immunised (World Bank (WHO/UNICEF))
  • Out-of-pocket health costs — % of total health spending (World Bank (WHO))
  • Civil service & NHS workforce turnover — Rolling 12-month leaver rate (NHS Digital workforce statistics)
  • Health & social care vacancy rate — Vacancies per 100 employee jobs, Human Health & Social Work sector (ONS JPB9) (ONS Labour Market Statistics (JPB9))
  • Life expectancy at birth — Years, England, both sexes (ONS national life tables)
