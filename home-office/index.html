Home Office

Operational throughput on the highest-volume, highest-salience flows: asylum, visas, and the costs of contingency.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £21bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Pending asylum seekers — Total pending in UK (UNHCR, all stages & origins) (UNHCR Refugee Statistics)
  • Net migration — Long-term international net migration, UK, year ending (ONS, Long-term international migration, provisional)
  • Crimes resulting in a charge — % of recorded offences with a charge or summons (Home Office, police recorded crime & outcomes open data)
  • Shoplifting offences — Police-recorded shoplifting, England & Wales, per year (ONS / Home Office police-recorded crime (Crime in England & Wales appendix tables))
  • Asylum caseworker turnover — 12-month rolling leaver rate (Home Office workforce data / NAO)
  • Asylum hotel contingency spend — Daily run-rate, £ million per day (Home Office accounts / NAO asylum costs)
  • Visa processing service standard — % of decisions within published service standard (UK Visas & Immigration transparency data)
  • Passport processing — % of straightforward passport applications within the service standard (HM Passport Office transparency data)
  • Homicide rate — Intentional homicides per 100,000 people — UK vs Germany & France (World Bank (UNODC))
  • Foreign-born population — International migrant stock, % of population (World Bank (UN))
