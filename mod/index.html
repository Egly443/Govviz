Govviz / Defence

Department for Defence

Whether the armed forces are at the size, mix, and readiness that the National Security Strategy requires, and whether equipment programmes deliver on time and on budget.

Approximate Total Managed Expenditure: £56bn (HM Treasury, Public Spending Statistics (PESA), 2025–26).

Tracked indicators

  • Trained personnel shortfall — Deficit against full-time trade requirement (MoD quarterly service personnel statistics)
  • Critical trades voluntary outflow — Annual voluntary outflow rate, pinch-point trades (MoD service personnel statistics)
  • MoD major projects delivery confidence — % of MoD GMPP projects rated Amber/Red or Red (IPA/NISTA Government Major Projects Portfolio)
  • Force readiness index — Composite capability score (NAO/SDSR proxies) (NAO reports on force readiness (proxy))
  • Defence spending (% of GDP) — Military expenditure, % of GDP (World Bank (SIPRI))
  • Armed forces personnel — Total military personnel (World Bank (IISS))
